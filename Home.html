Welcome

The institute is affiliated to Directorate of the Technical Education and Industrial Training, Punjab. It is approved by State Board of Technical Education, and Industrial Training Punjab as well as National council of Vocational Training under Director General of Employment and Training, Govt. of India. The Certificates are issued by both of these government departments. Thus the courses are at par with Government Industrial Training Institutes in Punjab as well as the whole country.


The institute is running under the aegis of National Education Trust (Regd.). The trust Comprises of educationists, doctors, engineers and administrators of great acumen and expertise in their respective fields. The institute is situated at Mahilpur, a town having both urban and rural environments. The pollution free surroundings give an impetus to pursue the learning and training in an ideal location. Special attention is paid to discipline, punctuality and commitment to Train and Learn. Following this principle, the institute has established its name & fame in wide length and breadth of Punjab. The staff drawn from the concerned fields is well experienced and dedicated to impart the best of training and knowledge. The institute is well equipped with all the machinery, equipment and tools as per norms laid down by DGET New Delhi. After the untimely sad demise of the president Mrs. Ravinder Kaur the institute is being successfully run under the Presidentship of Mrs. Sonika Dania, an educationist and professor in English in a reputed college.
